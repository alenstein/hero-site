thetha ai privacy policy

Effective Date: April 6, 2025

thetha ai ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. this privacy policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our ai-powered language platform and related services (collectively, the "services"). please read this policy carefully. by accessing or using our services, you agree to the terms of this privacy policy.

01. information we collect

we collect information that you provide directly to us when you create an account, use our ai tools, or communicate with our support team. this may include:

  • check_circle identifiers (name, email address, company name)
  • check_circle account credentials and preferences
  • check_circle prompt history and ai interactions
  • check_circle billing information and transaction history

02. how we use your information

we utilize the data collected to power the electric pulse of our platform, specifically for:

platform optimization

using interaction data to refine our large language models and improve accuracy for all users.

security & safety

monitoring for fraudulent activity and ensuring compliance with our ethical ai guidelines.

personalized experience

tailoring ai responses based on your historical context and specific domain requirements.

03. data sovereignty & security

your data is encrypted both in transit and at rest. we implement high-end cryptographic standards to ensure that your proprietary prompts and sensitive interactions remain within your control. we do not sell your personal data to third-party brokers.

shield_lock

end-to-end protection

every request processed through thetha ai's infrastructure is isolated within a secure vault, preventing cross-tenant data leakage and ensuring maximum privacy for enterprise partners.

04. cookies and tracking

we use cookies and similar tracking technologies to track activity on our services and hold certain information. cookies are files with a small amount of data which may include an anonymous unique identifier. you can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.

05. third-party service providers

we may employ third-party companies and individuals to facilitate our services, to provide the services on our behalf, to perform service-related services, or to assist us in analyzing how our services are used. these third parties have access to your personal information only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

06. compliance with laws

we will disclose your personal information where required to do so by law or subpoena or if we believe that such action is necessary to comply with the law and the reasonable requests of law enforcement or to protect the security or integrity of our services.

07. business transaction

if thetha ai is involved in a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your personal information may be transferred. we will provide notice before your personal information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

08. children's privacy

our services do not address anyone under the age of 18 ("children"). we do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 18. if you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

09. changes to this policy

we may update our privacy policy from time to time. we will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "effective date" at the top of this policy.

10. user rights

depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to access, correct, or delete the personal data we hold about you.

11. international data transfer

your information, including personal information, may be transferred to — and maintained on — computers located outside of your state, province, country, or other governmental jurisdiction where the data protection laws may differ from those of your jurisdiction.

12. data retention

we will retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy policy. we will retain and use your information to the extent necessary to comply with our legal obligations.
